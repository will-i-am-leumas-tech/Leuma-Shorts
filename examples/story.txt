I found the strangest post on Reddit last night. A guy said he bought an old arcade cabinet for twenty dollars, dragged it home, and found a folded note taped inside the back panel. The note warned him not to plug it in after midnight. Naturally, he ignored that advice.

At exactly twelve oh three, the machine powered on by itself. The screen flashed a scoreboard that listed his full name, his street, and the date of the next morning. Then the cabinet started printing receipts with short descriptions of things that had not happened yet.

The first receipt said he would spill coffee on a blue shirt. Ten minutes later he did. The second said his upstairs neighbor would knock on the door asking for a flashlight. She did that too. By sunrise he had a stack of receipts and absolutely no interest in finding out what the last one meant.
